We bring new life to your car's interior, wheels, and rims...and at an affordable rate!


AAA Detailing by Andrew Thibodeau, is a mobile auto detailing business serving customers in the Greater Boston Area. Andrew is a meticulous cleaner who pays close attention to detail and doesn't finish a job until it is 100% regardless of time or other circumstances.

Our Services

Interior Package

We only use the best products to clean and detail our clients’ cars. Interior package includes:

  • Full interior and trunk vacuum
  • Windows, windshield, and rear cleaned Inside and out
  • Full interior clean and protectant including front dash, radio, instru. clusters, and air vents.
  • All door panels, door jams, center console, and any other plastic, vinyl, or leather trim.
  • Floor mats are removed, vacuumed, and cleaned.
  • Weathertech or other vinyl floor mats are removed and cleaned with Meguiar's rubber/plastic cleaner.
  • Leather seats are cleaned and conditioned using Lexol leather cleaning solution and microfiber cloths.
  • Some stains can be removed from carpets, but we do not use a steamer.

Wheels and Tires

We only use the best products to clean and detail our clients' wheels and tires. We use Meguiar’s wheel and tire cleaner to breakdown and remove the dirt and grime from the wheels and then apply Meguiar's tire shine.
